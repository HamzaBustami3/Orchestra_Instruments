Woodwind Instruments

Woodwind instruments are instruments that are usually made of metal, wood, or plastic, but used to be made only of wood, and why it is called "woodwdind instruments" they are instruments that are played via plowing or air flow that is run through the instrument that is warped into a tone or sound.
Here are a few examples of woodwind instruments
  • Clarinet
  • Flute
  • Oboe
  • Bassoon
  • Saxophone
  • Piccolo
