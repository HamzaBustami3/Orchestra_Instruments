Strings

String instruments are played by drawing a bow across a set of strings, or by plucking the strings with the fingers.

  • Violin
  • Viola
  • Cello
  • Double Bass
  • Harp

Woodwinds

Woodwind instruments are played by blowing air through a mouthpiece or reed, which causes the air to vibrate and produce sound.

  • Flute
  • Clarinet
  • Oboe
  • Bassoon
  • Saxophone

Brass

Brass instruments are played by buzzing the lips into a mouthpiece, which causes the air to vibrate and produce sound.

  • Trumpet
  • Trombone
  • French Horn
  • Tuba

Percussion

Percussion instruments are played by striking or shaking them, and can produce a wide range of sounds and rhythms.

  • Timpani
  • Xylophone
  • Marimba
  • Glockenspiel
  • Cymbals
  • Snare Drum
  • Bass Drum
